Here at Blue Beach Vibes we help people achieve peace through meditation, yoga and the power of the beach. We know how big of an impact each of these tools can have on a person, so we decided to join the three together. Bringing your feelings to a center with meditation, then balancing that center out with yoga all while smelling and listening to the ocean can bring peace to anyone looking for it.

Beach Power

Great big bodies of water are known to have major impacts on a human body, spirit and mind. Here are a few interesting facts about how the ocean can affect humans in a positive way:

  • The slow, rhythmic sound of waves destresses the nervous system.
  • As a color, the blue ocean provides a calming sensation when observed.
  • The negative ions that float up with a sea breeze actually calm a brain. Studies have proven this calming effect is evident with people suffering from Seasonal Affective Disorder (SAD).
